How to add a new staff member

  1. Open the Supabase → Authentication → Users page.
  2. Click "Add user" → "Create new user". Enter the staff member's email and a temporary password. Tick "Auto Confirm User".
  3. Return here and refresh — the new user appears in the list below. Set their full name and role, then click Save.
  4. Share the email + temporary password with the staff member. They sign in at the login page.
